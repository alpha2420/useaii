================================================================================
                    CONVERRA AI - CRM SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE REPORT
================================================================================

1. OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Converra AI is a high-performance, human-in-the-loop CRM system designed to
automate business-to-customer communication via WhatsApp. It combines 
Real-time messaging, AI automation, and advanced lead intelligence.

2. CORE SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The system operates as two independent engines connected by a shared MongoDB
database:

A. WEB DASHBOARD (The Face)
   - Technologies: Next.js 16, Tailwind CSS, TypeScript.
   - Purpose: Management interface for business owners to see chats,
     configure AI settings, and manage their knowledge base.
   - Live Updates: Uses "optimistic UI" and polling to show messages the 
     moment they arrive.

B. BACKGROUND WORKER (The Brain)
   - Technologies: Node.js, WhatsApp-Web.js, Puppeteer.
   - Purpose: Runs 24/7 on the VPS. Handles the active WhatsApp connection,
     listens for messages, calls AI, and sends replies.

3. THE AI REPLIER WORKFLOW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
When a customer sends a message:
1. Capture: Worker detects the message.
2. Pre-process: Cleans slang, emojis, and filler words to save AI costs.
3. Cache Check: If the same question was asked before, sends a saved answer
   (0.0ms delay, $0 cost).
4. Smart Routing: Calls OpenAI (GPT-4o). If OpenAI is down, instantly 
   switches to Google Gemini Flash for 100% uptime.
5. Guardrails: If the AI is unsure (Low Confidence), it creates an 
   "Unanswered Question" log for the owner instead of hallucinating.

4. CRM INTELLIGENCE & MEMORY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The CRM does not just "chat"; it builds a profile for every contact:
- AI Extraction: Automatically identifies and saves the Customer's Name,
  Budget, Location, and specific needs.
- Lead Scoring: Categorizes every user as "Hot," "Warm," or "Cold" based
  on their buying intent.
- AI Summarization: Generates a 1-sentence summary of the entire chat
  history so the owner can catch up in seconds.

5. CONTROL MODES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- AUTOMATED MODE: AI handles everything based on the Knowledge Base.
- MANUAL MODE (AI Paused): The owner can "Pause AI" with one click to take
  over the conversation manually. The dashboard allows the owner to send
  messages through the bot's WhatsApp session.

6. DATA & SECURITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Persistence: All messages and analysis are stored in MongoDB.
- Usage Tracking: A Super Admin portal monitors token consumption per user
  to manage operational costs and credit balances.
- Role-Based Access: Secure authentication ensures only authorized owners
  can see their private business data.
